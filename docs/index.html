DemandVitals — Integrated Brand + Demand Marketing Website

A content-rich marketing site for B2B Tech/SaaS that explains how to build a modern demand engine by balancing long-term brand with short-term demand activation.

I designed and built the DemandVitals website to communicate a clear point of view on B2B growth while keeping performance, readability, and mobile UX front and center. The site guides visitors through the problem (why B2B marketing is breaking), the perspective (brand and demand must work together), and the solutions (Marketing Audit, Brand Marketing, Demand Activation, SearchX, RevOps).

The information architecture emphasizes progressive disclosure: high-level narrative first, then scannable sections with supportive visuals and CTAs. I structured content for maintainability, used semantic HTML for accessibility, and optimized images, SVGs, and typography for speed and clarity.

  • Sections: Why B2B marketing is breaking, The Reality, The DemandVitals Perspective, Solutions, Tech Stack, and Why DemandVitals.
  • Clear CTAs to book a GTM assessment and connect via LinkedIn.
  • Accessible, responsive layout with focus states and keyboard-friendly navigation.
  • Performance optimizations: lightweight assets, SVG icons, lazy visuals, and font loading best practices.
